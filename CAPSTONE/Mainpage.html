Arduino-based Automated Silken Tofu Maker: SMS-Based Notification, Soya Milk Cooker and Stirrer, and Calcium Sulfate Dispenser for the Silken Tofu Business of Rosa A. Carreon

The proposed prototype aims to seamlessly abstract the multiple steps and processes needed for the processing of silken tofu, specifically, for silken tofu production. The prototype would automate the process of making the silken tofu grinding and pulp separation, cooking and stirring the soya milk, to measuring and dispensing calcium sulfate.
